## AI for Grant Writing

A comprehensive curated list of resources for using Large Language Models (LLMs) to develop more competitive grant applications. This repository provides guidance, tools, and best practices for researchers looking to leverage AI in their grant writing process.

**Features:**
- Curated resources for AI-assisted grant writing
- Best practices for using LLMs in proposal development
- Tools and prompts for improving grant competitiveness
- Guidance on ethical AI use in academic writing

**Topics Covered:**
- Generative AI for scientific writing
- Grant proposal development strategies
- LLM prompt engineering for grants
