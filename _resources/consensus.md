## Consensus

Consensus is an AI search engine designed specifically for research. It searches 200M+ peer-reviewed papers and provides evidence-based answers with citation-backed summaries.

**Features:**
- Consensus Copilot for instant topic summaries
- Consensus Meter showing research agreement levels
- Direct search of peer-reviewed literature
- Citation export and integration
