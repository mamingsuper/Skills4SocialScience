## ResearchRabbit

ResearchRabbit helps researchers discover new papers by building personalized recommendation networks from seed papers. It learns your interests over time to surface increasingly relevant work.

**Features:**
- Interactive citation network visualization
- Personalized paper recommendations that improve over time
- Collection-based organization of research threads
- Free to use for all researchers
