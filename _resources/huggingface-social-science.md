## HuggingFace Social Science Datasets

HuggingFace Hub hosts thousands of datasets relevant to social science NLP research, including sentiment analysis, political text classification, hate speech detection, and survey response data.

**Highlights:**
- Ready-to-use datasets with standardized formats
- Integration with Python `datasets` library
- Community-contributed social science datasets
- Model cards with benchmark results
