## Litmaps

Litmaps creates interactive, visual maps of academic literature showing citation relationships and temporal evolution of research topics.

**Features:**
- Dynamic citation maps with timeline views
- DOI-based paper search and discovery
- Export to BibTeX, CSV, RIS formats
- Zotero sync for Pro users
- Shareable public map URLs for collaboration
