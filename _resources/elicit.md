## Elicit

Elicit is an AI-powered research assistant that uses LLMs to help researchers find, summarize, and extract structured data from academic papers at scale.

**Features:**
- Semantic search across 138M+ academic papers
- Automated data extraction tables from unstructured papers
- Systematic review screening and analysis
- PDF upload and chat-with-papers functionality
- Zotero integration for citation management
