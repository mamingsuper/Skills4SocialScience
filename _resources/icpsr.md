## ICPSR Data Archive

The Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research (ICPSR), based at the University of Michigan, is the world's largest repository of social and behavioral science data.

**Features:**
- 16,000+ curated studies spanning decades
- Survey data, census data, experimental data
- Online data analysis tools
- DOI-based data citation support
- Free access for member institutions
