# AI and the Transformation of Social Science Research

Grossmann et al. provide a concise overview of AI's transformative potential across social science disciplines, published in *Science*, Vol. 380, Issue 6650 (2023).

**Key themes:**
- AI as a catalyst for scaling qualitative and quantitative research
- Opportunities for cross-disciplinary collaboration enabled by AI
- Risks of methodological shortcuts and interpretive over-reliance on AI
