ü§ñ What Are AI Coding Agents?

AI coding agents (like Claude Code, Cursor, or Gemini CLI) are intelligent assistants that live in your terminal or editor. Unlike ChatGPT, they can:

üìÇ

Read Your Project

They can scan your entire folder, understanding your data structure, paper drafts, and code.

‚ö°

Execute Commands

They can run Python scripts, install R packages, or compile LaTeX documents autonomously.

üß†

Follow Context

They remember your research question and methodology throughout the session.

üõ†Ô∏è What Are "Skills"?

Skills are specialized instruction sets that teach an AI agent how to perform a specific research task perfectly. Think of them as "Standard Operating Procedures" (SOPs) for AI.

# Without a Skill: User: "Run a regression." AI: *Writes generic code that might ignore robust standard errors.* # With the 'stata-regression' Skill: User: "/stata-regression Run a model on my panel data." AI: *Automatically uses reghdfe, clusters standard errors, and formats output for publication.*

By using skills from this repository, you ensure your AI assistant follows best practices in social science methodology.
