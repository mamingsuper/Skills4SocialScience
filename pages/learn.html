Understanding AI for Research

How to use AI Agents, Skills, and Workflows to accelerate your social science research.

ü§ñ What Are AI Coding Agents?

AI coding agents (like Claude Code, Cursor, or Gemini CLI) are intelligent assistants that live in your terminal or editor. Unlike ChatGPT, they can:

  • Read your entire folder, understanding data structure, paper drafts, and code
  • Execute Python scripts, install R packages, or compile LaTeX documents
  • Remember your research question and methodology throughout the session

üõ†Ô∏è What Are "Skills"?

Skills are specialized instruction sets that teach an AI agent how to perform a specific research task perfectly.

# Without a Skill: User: "Run a regression." AI: *Writes generic code* # With the Skill: User: "/stata-regression Run panel data model" AI: *Uses reghdfe, clusters standard errors*

üîÑ The AI Research Workflow

We organize tools by the research lifecycle:

  • Literature Review - AI summarizes papers, extracts arguments
  • Data & Analysis - Automate cleaning, modeling, visualization
  • Writing & Publishing - Draft sections, format citations, convert to LaTeX
