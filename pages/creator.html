How to Contribute

1
Choose Your Contribution Type

Decide what you'd like to share with the community:

  • AI Skills: Prompt templates for AI agents (SKILL.md files)
  • Papers: Curated academic papers with annotations
  • Tools: GitHub repositories, datasets, or research platforms
  • Tutorials: Step-by-step guides for specific workflows

2
Prepare Your Content

Ensure your contribution follows our quality standards:

  • For Skills: Create a well-structured SKILL.md with clear instructions
  • For Papers: Include title, authors, year, journal, and DOI/PDF link
  • For Tools: Provide description, GitHub URL, and categorization
  • Include relevant tags and research workflow stage

3
Submit via Form or GitHub

Share your contribution through your preferred channel:

  • Use our online submission form for quick submissions
  • Open a Pull Request on GitHub for direct contribution
  • Email us for bulk submissions or special cases

4
Review & Publication

Our team reviews submissions for quality and relevance:

  • Submissions are typically reviewed within 1-2 weeks
  • We may suggest edits for clarity or completeness
  • Accepted contributions are published with your attribution
  • You'll be notified when your contribution goes live
