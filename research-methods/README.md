# Research Methods

This section provides guidance on various research methodologies used in social science.

## Contents

### Quantitative Research Methods
- **Survey Design**: Questionnaire development, sampling, response rates
- **Experimental Design**: RCTs, quasi-experiments, A/B testing
- **Secondary Data Analysis**: Using existing datasets
- **Measurement**: Validity, reliability, scale development

### Qualitative Research Methods
- **Interviews**: Semi-structured, structured, unstructured
- **Focus Groups**: Planning, moderation, analysis
- **Ethnography**: Participant observation, field notes
- **Case Studies**: Design, data collection, analysis
- **Content Analysis**: Coding, themes, interpretation

### Mixed Methods Research
- **Integration Strategies**: Convergent, explanatory, exploratory
- **Design Types**: Sequential, concurrent, transformative
- **Analysis**: Combining qualitative and quantitative data

## Research Design
1. Define research questions
2. Choose appropriate methodology
3. Plan data collection
4. Consider ethics and validity
5. Prepare analysis strategy

## Ethics in Research
- Informed consent
- Confidentiality and anonymity
- Data protection
- Ethical approval processes
