# Survey Design Helper

This skill assists researchers in designing rigorous survey instruments following established survey methodology best practices.

## Capabilities
- **Question Design**: Draft clear, unbiased survey questions avoiding double-barreled or leading items
- **Response Scales**: Recommend appropriate Likert, semantic differential, or open-ended formats
- **Skip Logic**: Design branching logic for complex survey flows
- **Cognitive Pretesting**: Simulate respondent comprehension to identify problematic items
- **Translation**: Maintain measurement equivalence across languages

## Usage
```
/survey-design Review my questionnaire for political trust and suggest improvements
```

## Methodological Standards
- Follows Dillman's Tailored Design Method principles
- Applies AAPOR best practices for question wording
- Checks for acquiescence bias, social desirability, and order effects
