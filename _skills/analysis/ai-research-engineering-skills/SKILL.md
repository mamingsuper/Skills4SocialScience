# AI Research Engineering Skills Library

The most comprehensive open-source library of AI research engineering skills for AI agents.

## Source
- GitHub: https://github.com/Orchestra-Research/AI-Research-SKILLs

## Description
This library provides a collection of research engineering skills designed for AI agents, covering various aspects of AI research including code execution, experiment tracking, paper reading, and more.

## Use Cases
- AI research automation
- Experiment management and tracking
- Paper analysis and summarization
- Code development and debugging
- Research workflow optimization
